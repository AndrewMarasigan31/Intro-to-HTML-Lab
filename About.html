About Us

Our mission is to become the number #1 Coffee Shop of affordable and extensive coffee here in the Philippines.

Digital Expresso is the all-in one stop for all your coffee Needs. Started out as a thesis project, the owners Dave,Jerry, and Michael has allowed Digital Expresso to serve over 100,000 customers in Visayas and Luzon. Majority of the customers we serve are millenials and young teens who want ready to serve coffee at affordable prices. We serve premium and extensive coffee products to cater to any of your cravings.
